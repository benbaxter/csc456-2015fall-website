Continious Integration

Continious Integration is a crucial part of streamlining development. Setup an CI server and document the process.

By using git for other homeworks, you already have a head start. There are free tools like Jenkins that can help orchestrate everything.

In your report, include the following items:
  • Intro to CI
  • The benifit of CI
  • The downsides of CI
  • Which tools did you use to build out your CI Server?
  • How does your CI work?
In addition, you will be required to give a small demostration of your CI Server.
